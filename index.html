How to make tea

At the very lat, tea is a flavourful way of getting enough fluid into your body every day. On top of that, studies have shown teas can help protect your teeth and your heart

Ingredients

  • Tea Bags
  • Water
  • Milk-Dairy/Plant-Based (Optional)
  • Sugar/Honey (Optional)

Preparation

  1. Run the tab a little so the water's nicely aerated, and only boil it once to keep the oxygen level up. Oxygen in water help flavour!
  2. POP a tea bag into your mug
  3. Pour the hot water over the tea bag and stir briefly
  4. Tea needs time to unlock all its flavours, so give it 3-4 minutes to do its things. This is a perfect time to grab a sneaky cookie or daydream about vacations
  5. Before removing the tea bag, gently squish it with a spoon against the side of the mug. Not too much or you'll make it bitter
  6. If you want, throw in some milk or sugar or honey or nothing else at all
  7. Enjoy!
